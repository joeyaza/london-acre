About


London Acre is a dynamic property development company specialising in creating boutique residential schemes in key locations across the capital.

Design, quality and attention to detail lie at the heart of the company’s ethos, ensuring that every London Acre home is created with its residents in mind.

The company, which was founded in early 2016, was created in response to the growing demand for thoughtfully designed, well-located homes that meet and exceed the needs of London’s rapidly expanding population.

London Acre’s team has a proven track record achieving planning permission for a number of high profile London schemes. Working with industry leading architects, designers and consultants, a tailored approach is taken to each site, from the point of acquisition through to the design and delivery of these exceptional properties.

Managing Director, Ross Jacobson, has in-depth knowledge and extensive experience of London’s residential and commercial property markets, having overseen in excess of £100 million of property transactions in the capital.
