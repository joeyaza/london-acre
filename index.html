ABOUT

LONDON ACRE IS A DYNAMIC PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT COMPANY SPECIALISING IN CREATING BOUTIQUE RESIDENTIAL SCHEMES IN KEY LOCATIONS ACROSS THE CAPITAL.

DESIGN, QUALITY AND ATTENTION TO DETAIL LIE AT THE HEART OF THE COMPANY’S ETHOS, ENSURING THAT EVERY LONDON ACRE HOME IS CREATED WITH ITS RESIDENTS IN MIND.

THE COMPANY, FOUNDED IN EARLY 2016, WAS CREATED IN RESPONSE TO THE GROWING DEMAND FOR THOUGHTFULLY DESIGNED, WELL-LOCATED HOMES THAT MEET AND EXCEED THE NEEDS OF LONDON’S RAPIDLY EXPANDING POPULATION.

LONDON ACRE’S TEAM HAS A PROVEN TRACK RECORD FOR ACHIEVING PLANNING PERMISSION FOR A NUMBER OF HIGH PROFILE LONDON SCHEMES. WORKING WITH INDUSTRY-LEADING ARCHITECTS, DESIGNERS AND CONSULTANTS, A TAILORED APPROACH IS TAKEN TO EACH SITE, FROM THE POINT OF ACQUISITION THROUGH TO THE DESIGN AND DELIVERY OF THESE EXCEPTIONAL PROPERTIES.

MANAGING DIRECTOR, ROSS JACOBSON, HAS IN-DEPTH KNOWLEDGE AND EXTENSIVE EXPERIENCE OF LONDON’S RESIDENTIAL AND COMMERCIAL PROPERTY MARKETS, HAVING OVERSEEN IN EXCESS OF £100 MILLION OF PROPERTY TRANSACTIONS IN THE CAPITAL.

REQUIREMENTS

LONDON ACRE IS SEEKING RESIDENTIAL DEVELOPMENT OPPORTUNITIES IN KEY CENTRAL LONDON LOCATIONS THAT MEET THE FOLLOWING REQUIREMENTS:

  • UNCONDITIONAL, CONSENTED AND SUBJECT TO PLANNING RESIDENTIAL-LED SCHEMES
  • MULTI-UNIT APARTMENT AND HOUSING SCHEMES WILL BE CONSIDERED
  • LOCATED WITHIN LONDON ZONES 1-5
  • PURCHASE PRICES RANGING FROM £1-5 MILLION
  • SALES VALUES FROM £500-1500 PER SQ FT
  • PROXIMITY TO EXCELLENT TRANSPORT LINKS IS A PREREQUISITE

IN A FAST-PACED MARKET THAT RELIES ON THE ABILITY TO TRANSACT QUICKLY, LONDON ACRE IS WELL-POSITIONED TO DRAW ON ITS OWN IN-HOUSE FUNDS, ENABLING THE COMPANY TO EXCHANGE AND COMPLETE ON SITE ACQUISITIONS WITHIN SHORT TIMEFRAMES.
